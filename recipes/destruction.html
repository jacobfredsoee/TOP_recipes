Recipe for destruction

While not everyone's favorite thing, some people really do like a good destruction.

Ingredients

  • 3 anarchists
  • 2 molotov cocktails
  • 10% luck
  • 20% skill
  • 15% concentrated power of will
  • 5% pleasure
  • 50% pain

Directions

  1. Have the 3 anarchists meet (pref. underground) to plan overthrowing the evil people in power
  2. Write manifest of changes
  3. Assemble molotov cocktails
  4. Combine the luck, skill and concentrated power of will
  5. Now destroy the symbol of power
  6. Experience the pleasure and pain (results may vary) and 100% reason to remember the name

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 0-1500 calories.
